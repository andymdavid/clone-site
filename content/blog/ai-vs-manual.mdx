Manual workflows break down once you scale past a handful of operators. AI-first flows excel because they:

- run **24/7** without overtime
- leave an audit trail every time
- learn from feedback loops

Manual work can still win when empathy or complex judgment is required, but pairing humans with automation gives you the best of both worlds.
